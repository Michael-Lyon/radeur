Welcome to Radeur

Radeur is a community-driven, open-source project dedicated to simplifying the search for reliable internet service providers in Nigeria.

Faced with the challenge of choosing the right network in a specific area, many of us end up trying multiple ISPs before finding a suitable match. Radeur aims to alleviate this hassle. By collecting and showcasing user ratings for different network providers, along with their service locations, we empower you to make informed decisions based on collective experiences.
